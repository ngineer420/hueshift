Privacy

hueshift.net is a set of color tools that run entirely in your browser. This page explains, plainly, what that means for your data.

What we don't collect

hueshift.net has no server-side application, no account system, and no analytics beacons. The colors you pick, the gradients you build, and any image you drop into the Image Color Extractor are processed with JavaScript already running in your tab — none of it is ever sent to us, because there's no endpoint for it to go to.

Images you upload

The Image Color Extractor reads image files using your browser's own File and Canvas APIs. The file never leaves your device — there is no upload step, and closing or refreshing the tab discards it completely.

Local storage

hueshift.net saves two small preferences in your browser's local storage: your light/dark theme choice and your chosen accent color. These stay on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. You can clear them at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser settings.

Advertising

This site shows ads served by Google AdSense, which may use cookies to personalize ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can control ad personalization through Google's Ad Settings, and learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
